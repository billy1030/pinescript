# Smart Volume Profile (SVP) — User Guide

Welcome to the **Smart Volume Profile (SVP)** indicator! This professional-grade tool goes beyond standard volume profiles by detecting hidden institutional footprints, explosive breakout zones, and dynamic support/resistance levels.

It is highly optimized for trading volatile **high-beta stocks** (like SOXL, TQQQ, NVDA) and serves as the perfect companion to the AKRTS indicator.

---

## 1. Core Visuals: What You See on the Chart

### 📊 The Histogram (Right Side)
*   **Teal Bars (Buy Volume):** Shows volume that occurred when the candle closed higher than it opened at this price level.
*   **Red Bars (Sell Volume):** Shows volume that occurred when the candle closed lower than it opened.
*   *How to use:* Look for levels where one color completely dominates the other to find hidden accumulation or distribution.

### 🟡 The Yellow Line (POC - Point of Control)
*   This is the exact price level with the highest traded volume in the lookback period.
*   *How to use:* The POC acts as a **strong magnet / fair value**. If price moves far away from it, it has a high statistical probability of returning to this line.

### 🔴 The Red & Green Dashed Lines (VAH & VAL)
*   **Value Area High (VAH - Red):** The upper boundary of where 70% of the volume traded.
*   **Value Area Low (VAL - Green):** The lower boundary of where 70% of the volume traded.
*   *How to use:* These act as the "walls" of a trading range. A breakout above VAH or a breakdown below VAL often signifies a powerful trend resuming.

## 2. Advanced Zones (Smart Money Footprints)

### 🟧 Orange Zones (LVN - Low Volume Nodes)
*   These are price zones with extremely low trading volume (air pockets).
*   *How to use:* Because there are few resting orders here, **price accelerates violently** through orange zones. If price enters an orange zone, expect a fast, frictionless move to the other side.

### 🟩 Bright Green Boxes (Buy Imbalance) & 🟥 Bright Red Boxes (Sell Imbalance)
*   These highlight price levels where an abnormal imbalance occurred (e.g., >70% buy volume vs sell volume).
*   *How to use:* 
    *   **Green boxes below you** act as an **Institutional Floor** (strong support). Institutions bought heavily here and will likely defend it.
    *   **Red boxes above you** act as an **Institutional Ceiling** (strong resistance). Institutions sold heavily here.

---

## 3. Key Levels & Magnets

### ⏸️ Thick Gray Lines (Auto Support / Resistance)
*   The algorithm scans the histogram to find the "peaks of the mountains" (High Volume Nodes) and automatically draws horizontal lines.
*   *How to use:* Trade these exactly like traditional S/R lines. They are areas of high liquidity.

### 📍 Dotted Orange Lines (Naked POC / nPOC)
*   These are POCs from previous days that price *never came back down/up to touch*. 
*   *How to use:* These act as **extreme price magnets**. If you are in a winning trade, target the nearest Naked POC as your Take Profit level.

### 📘 Blue (Daily) & 🟪 Purple (Weekly) Lines (Multi-TF POC)
*   These show where the Fair Value is resting on the Daily and Weekly charts.
*   *How to use:* If you see a Daily POC line overlapping with your current intraday POC, this is a **Confluence Zone** — extremely powerful support/resistance.

## 5. How to use SVP with AKRTS

Use the two indicators together like a sniper setup:
*   **SVP gives you the WHERE.** (The battlefield map, S/R lines, acceleration zones).
*   **AKRTS gives you the WHEN.** (The precise moment to pull the trigger: `BUY 100%`).

**The Golden Setup:**
1. You see a thick gray Auto S/R line holding the price up, OR price drops into a bright Green Buy Imbalance zone.
2. You look above and see an orange LVN zone (a fast highway).
3. **AKRTS fires a `BUY 100%` signal.**
4. You take the trade, expecting the price to bounce off the strong SVP floor and accelerate rapidly through the orange LVN highway up to the nearest Naked POC.
